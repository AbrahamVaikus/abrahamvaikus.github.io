Both edges of a musical work are inhabited by silence. The most graceful and noble mission of music must be to enhance the beauty of silence. The works of Abraham Vaikus stand as elements that threaten silence, interspersing facts taken from the world that surrounds us in all their rawness.

Silence is the most sublime of all beauties, inasmuch as it is the only one that enables us to contemplate all the others in abundance of fullness. Endowing our life with a deep spiritual dimension is the path to personal fulfillment. Besieged by noise, let the silence on both edges of the music guide you.

Abraham Vaikus does exist and is usually silent.
